About the Game

Area 12 is a premier, high-fidelity sandbox construction and multiplayer warfare gaming franchise. Blending deep, community-driven survival architectures, modular builder modes, and responsive player-versus-player (PvP) match configurations, the franchise represents the pinnacle of creative cooperative gameplay.
